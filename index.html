What is this?

This is a project built by Kevin Owocki at Zuzalu.

Inspired by
1. my time here
2. the philosophy of Practical Pluralism
3. my recent podcast episode with Vitalik , where he explains that public goods funding is 1 part mechanism and 1 part funding source

... I am trying to build a visual showing how funding from different funding sources is trickling through the ethereum ecosystem.

I tweeted about this concept in this tweet thread, and it seems that others were interested in it too.

So I started building it for the ZuConnect Hackathon.
